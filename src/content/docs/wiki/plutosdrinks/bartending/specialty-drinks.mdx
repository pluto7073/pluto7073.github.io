:::note
The contents of this page apply to [The Art of Bartending](https://modrinth.com/mod/bartending)
:::

Below is a list of every specialty drink added by The Art of Bartending, as well as its recipe.

## Appletini
**Base**: Any [empty glass](glassware)
- 1x [Ice](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ice)
- 4x [Shot of Vodka](alcohol-shot)
- 2x Shot of Apple Liqueur
- 1x [Sugar](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Sugar)
- 1x [Apple](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Apple)

## Kamikaze
:::note
Requires [Fruitful Fun](https://modrinth.com/mod/fruitful-fun)
:::

**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Shot of Vodka
- 1x Shot of Orange Liqueur
- 1x Lime

## Margarita
:::note
Note: Requires Fruitful Fun
:::

**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Ice
- 2x Shot of Tequila
- 1x Shot of Orange Liqueur
- 1x Lime

## Mimosa
:::note
Note: Requires Fruitful Fun
:::

**Base**: [Glass of Champagne](alcohol-glass)
- 1x Orange

## Espresso Martini
:::note
Requires [Pluto's Coffee Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/plutos-coffee-mod)
:::

**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Shot of Vodka
- 1x Shot of Coffee Liqueur
- 1x [Shot of Espresso](/wiki/plutosdrinks/plutoscoffee/espresso-machine)
- 1x Sugar
- 1x [Cocoa Beans](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cocoa_Beans)
- 1x [Roasted Coffee Bean](/wiki/plutosdrinks/plutoscoffee/roast-coffee-beans)

## Bourbon Sweet Tea
:::note
Requires [Tea Time](https://modrinth.com/mod/teatime)
:::

**Base**: Black [Brewed Tea](/wiki/plutosdrinks/teatime/brewed-tea)
- 3x Sugar
- 1x Shot of Whiskey
- 1x Ice

## Earl Grey Old Fashioned
:::note
Requires Tea Time and Fruitful Fun
:::

**Base**: Any empty glass
- 2x Shot of Whiskey
- 1x Sugar
- 2x Earl Grey [Tea Bag](/wiki/plutosdrinks/teatime/tea-bag)
- 1x Ice
- 1x Orange

## Death In The Afternoon
**Base**: Glass of Champagne
- 1x Shot of Absinthe

## Manhattan
**Base**: Any empty glass
- 2x Shot of Whiskey
- 1x Shot of Sweet Vermouth

## Martini
**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Ice
- 1x Shot of Dry Vermouth
- 3x Shot of Gin

## Old Fashioned
**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Sugar
- 1x Shot of Whiskey
- 1x Sweet Berries

## Vodka Martini
**Base**: Any empty glass
- 1x Ice
- 3x Shot of Vodka
- 1x Shot of Dry Vermouth
