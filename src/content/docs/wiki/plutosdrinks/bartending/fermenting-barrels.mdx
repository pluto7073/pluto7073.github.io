Fermenting barrels are special types of barrels that can be used to age alcoholic drinks

## Obtaining

### Breaking
A fermenting barrel can be mined with or without any tool, but an [axe](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Axe) is the quickest.  When broken, it drops all of its contents.

## Usage

### Barrel Aging
To use a fermenting barrel, simply place one and put the item you wish to age (either a concoction or a bottle of alcohol) in the barrel.  By default, 1 year is 3 Minecraft days.  This can be changed via the [config](/wiki/plutosdrinks/config).
