All alcoholic drinks should be stored in one of the above types of bottles, except Beer Bottles.  If a drink can
be stored in a beer bottle, it should also be stored in a Jug as the primary drink form of storage.  Beer Bottles
are filled the same as other glasses.

## Glasses

The Art of Bartending adds 5 new glasses:
- Tall Glass
- Short Glass
- Shot Glass
- Wine Glass
- Cocktail Glass

All of these can be crafted with a glass bottle in a stonecutter.

| Ingredients                                           | Stonecutting recipe                                                                                                                                 |
|-------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Glass Bottle](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Glass_bottle) | <img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/TheArtOfBartending-Fabric/blob/1.20/images/recipes/glassware.png?raw=true" width="200" alt="glass recipes"/> |

These glasses, except the shot glass and including the glass bottle, can also now be used as bases for specialty
drinks, turning into mixed drinks with a texture matching the base glass or bottle.

The Tall Glass, Wine Glass, and Cocktail glass can receive the below drinks:

| Tall Glass | Wine Glass                            | Cocktail Glass         |
|------------|---------------------------------------|------------------------|
| Any Beer   | Any Wine Except Nether Wine, Vermouth | Nether Wines, Absinthe |

The tall glass holds a starting value of 12 oz, while the wine and cocktail glasses hold starting sizes of 5 oz.
If used as a base for a mixed drink the starting volume is 0 oz.

The shot glass can be used to add alcohol to any other drink or alcohol recipe.
