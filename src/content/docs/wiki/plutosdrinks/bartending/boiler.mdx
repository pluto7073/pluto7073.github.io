A boiler is a block used for the first step in the alcohol brewing process, initial fermentation

## Usage

First, fill the boiler with water, either by using a bucket to fill all at once or by using a water pipe in Create or similar kinds of mods.  Next, add your main ingredients.  Ingredients can be found in game via
[REI](https://modrinth.com/rei) or via the [recipes](recipes) page.  Then the boiler must be heated.  This can be done by placing a heating block underneath the boiler.  This can be any type of fire or campfire, or
can be a blaze burner with create.

Then, once fermented for long enough, you may use a glass bottle to remove ¼ bucket of the concoction from the boiler (250 mB).

With create, the engineer's goggles will show information about what is currently boiling when looking at a boiler.
