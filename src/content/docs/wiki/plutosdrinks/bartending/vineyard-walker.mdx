**Vineyard Walker** is an enchantment applied to [leggings](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Leggings) that allows walking in bushes without taking damage

## Obtaining
Vineyard Walker can be obtained from [enchantment tables](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Enchantment_tables), books obtained by [fishing](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fishing), and librarian [villagers](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Villager).

## Usage
When walking in sweet berries, the players speed is usually reduced and the player will take damage when moving.  With vineyard walker this is not the case, and the player can walk though them as if it was a normal crop.  This enchantment also protects from damage taken from wither roses (more plant protection coming soon).
