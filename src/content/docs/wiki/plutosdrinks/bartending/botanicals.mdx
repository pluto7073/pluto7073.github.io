**Botanicals** are a type of item that consist of most plants that can be included in alcoholic drinks.  Below
is a list of standard botanical items.

This list is managed by the `bartending:botanical_elements` item tag and can be customized.

- Tree Bark (From [Farmer's Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/farmers-delight))
- [Hanging Roots](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hanging_roots)
- `minecraft:flowers` item tag
