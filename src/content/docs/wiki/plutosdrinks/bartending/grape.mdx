A **grape** is a [food](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Food) [item](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Item) obtained from grape crops that can be used to [make wine](recipes), eat, or [turn into seeds](#crafting)

**Grape Plants** are planted on a [vine frame](vine-frame) in [farmland](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland) and used to grow red and green grapes depending on the variant of the plant.

## Obtaining

### Breaking & Harvesting
Fully grown grape crops yield 1-9 grapes (4 ½ grapes per crop harvested on average).  Grape yield can be
increased using a tool enchanted with [Fortune](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fortune), with Fortune III the
average increases to ~6 ½ grapes per crop.

The first grape always drops, and then eight more attempts are made to drop a grape with a success rate
of 57.14286% to yield the extra 0-8 drops.  Each level of Fortune enchantment increases the number of attempts
by one.

## Usage

### Farming
When farmed, grapes go through 8 stages of growth on the bottom and 4 on the top. (Starting at index 0 for both,
bottom ages until 6, then top jumps to 3 and ages until 8, once at 7 the bottom also gets set to 7 and is
ready for harvest).  However, there are only 8 total visual stages, with 1-7 being the same for both red and
green grapes, and Stage 8 (index 7 for both) is where we see the color difference.

Grapes require a [light level](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Light) of 8 or greater to plant and 9 or greater to grow.
Crops grow faster if the farmland they are planted on is [hydrated](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Farmland#Hydration).
Using [bone meal](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Bone_meal) on crops also increases the speed of growth by randomly
increasing the growth stage 2-5 between the top and bottom.

Crops break if pushed by a [piston](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Piston) or if their supporting farmland breaks or
turns to dirt, dropping usual drops.

### Food
To eat a grape, press and hold `use` while it is selected in the [hotbatr](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Hotbar).
Eating a grape restores 1 hunger.

### Crafting
