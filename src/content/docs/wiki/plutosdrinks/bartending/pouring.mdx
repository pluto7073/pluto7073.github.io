---
title: "Pouring"
desc: "How to pour alcohol into various other drinks"
---

All alcoholic drink bottles can be poured into various other places.

To pour a drink, hold the empty item in your offhand and the bottle of alcohol in your main-hand.  Then hold
right-click until the animation finishes to pour into the off-hand slot.  If the empty glass or bottle in the
off-hand is not compatible with the drink in the main-hand, no animation will take place.

## Shot Glass
The following bottles can be poured into a shot glass.
- Absinthe
- Apple Liqueur
- Coffee Liqueur
- Dry Vermouth
- Gin
- Grain Alcohol
- Orange Liqueur
- Rum
- Sweet Vermouth
- Tequila
- Vodka
- Whiskey

## Tall Glass & Beer Bottle
- Beer
- Wheat Beer
- Dark Beer

## Wine Glass
- Mead
- Apple Mead
- Red Wine
- White Wine
- Sweet Berry Wine
- Glow Berry Wine
- Champagne
- Dry Vermouth
- Sweet Vermouth

## Cocktail Glass
- Crimson Wine
- Warped Wine
- Absinthe
