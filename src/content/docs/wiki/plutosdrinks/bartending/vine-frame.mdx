A vine frame is a block used to grow vine-like crops

## Obtaining

### Breaking

A vine frame are most quickly broken with an [axe](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Axe), but drop when broken with any tool.

## Usage
A vine frame can be used by right-clicking on the lower half with a matching seed.  Any mod can add their own instances of vine crops which are automatically added to the vine frame's functionality.

Additionally, you can craft a vine frame with one matching seed to get a placeable starter for the specified plant.  Right now, known plants that use vine frames are:
- [Grapes](grape)
