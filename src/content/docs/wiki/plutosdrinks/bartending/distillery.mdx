## Usage

To heat a distillery, you must first heat it similarly to how you heat a Boiler.  Using any kind of fire or campfire or a blaze burner with create.  Then, you place the concoction or another full bottle
of alcohol to distill it one time.  If the most recent brewer step is distilling, it adds one to that, if not, it creates a new step.
