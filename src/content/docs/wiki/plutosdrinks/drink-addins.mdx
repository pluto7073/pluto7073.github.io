Another way to customize your drinks is to add things to it.  This can be done with a [Drink Workstation](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosDrinksAPI/wiki/Drink-Workstation).

To craft a drink workstation, all you need is 2 stone blocks and 2 of any planks.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/coffee_workstation_recipe.png?raw=true" width="400"/>

To use, place any [modded drink base](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosDrinksAPI/wiki/Valid-Drink-Bases) in the first slot, your addition item in the second slot, and then the result will appear in the third slot with a tooltip stating the current additions.

Most additions also change the color of the drink that they are in.  Usually this reflects the item color.  The only exceptions are Sugar, Ice, and Honey.

## List of Additions

### Milk

In order to add milk to your drink, you must obtain a milk bottle, as we don't want to add an entire bucket of milk to your drink (unless you do? pop off I guess).
You can obtain a milk bottle either by crafting or by cows.  To craft a milk bottle, just place a milk bucket in the center of the crafting table with 4 glass bottles surrounding it.  This will split the bucket into the 4 bottles.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/milk_bottle.png?raw=true" width="400"/>

You can from there craft the 4 bottles back into a bucket by placing the empty bucket in the center surrounded by 4 milk bottles.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/milk_bucket.png?raw=true" width="400"/>

You can also use a glass bottle on a cow to receive a single milk bottle, similar to milking a cow with a bucket

Upon drinking something with milk, the user will be cleared of all negative status effects.

### Caramel (Pluto's Coffee Mod)

In order to add caramel to your drink you must craft a Bottle of Caramel.  This can be done by crafting a glass bottle with 6 sugar.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/caramel_recipe.png?raw=true" width="400"/>

Caramel is one of the most OP add-ins, as upon drinking a coffee or latte with caramel then the user will receive a 2-minute health boost of 6 Hearts, 2 Absorbtion hearts for 10 seconds, and Regeneration for 10 seconds.

### Sugar

Adding sugar to a drink will cause it to restore 1 hunger point

### Mocha Syrup (Pluto's Coffee Mod)

To craft mocha syrup, place a glass bottle in the bottom center slot, with the top six slots alternating 3 sugar and 3 cocoa beans, starting with a sugar.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/mocha_syrup_recipe.png?raw=true" width="400"/>

Adding mocha syrup to a drink will cause it to restore 6 hunger points

### Espresso Shot (Pluto's Coffee Mod)

An espresso shot can be pulled using the [Espresso Machine](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosDrinksAPI/wiki/Espresso-Machine).  Adding one to a drink will increase the caffeine content depending on the type.

Roast | Caffeine
---|---
Blonde | 85mg
Regular | 75mg
Decaf | 2mg

### Tea Bags (TeaTime AND PlutosCoffee)

Tea Bags from TeaTime can be added to Lattes from PlutosCoffee to create a Tea Latte.  This will give the drinker the same effects as drinking a brewed tea of the same tea type.

### Ice

Adding a block of ice to adrink will cause it to become iced, which grants the drinker 10 seconds of `Fire Resistance II` upon consumption.


### Chorus Fruit

Drinking something containing a chorus fruit will have the same effect as eating a chorus fruit.  It will teleport the player up to 8 blocks in any direction.


### Glow Berries

Drinking something containing glow berries will give the user the [Glowing](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Glowing) status effect for 1 minute.

### Honey

Honey has the same effect on drinks as sugar.  It will restore 1 hunger point to the player.

### Diamond

Diamonds have no actual effect on the drink they are added to, however the player is granted an advancement for drinking a coffee or latte with a diamond in it.

### Netherite

Like Diamonds, netherite has no actual effect on the drink other than granting an advancement to the player.
