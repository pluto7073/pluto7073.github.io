A **coffee brewer** is a block used for brewing plain coffee using coffee grounds and water.

## Obtaining
### Breaking
A coffee brewer can be obtained using any pickaxe.  It will not drop anything when mined without a pickaxe, excluding any items inside.

## Usage
> *Main Article: [Brewing Coffee](/wiki/plutosdrinks/plutoscoffee/brewing-coffee)*

Coffee Brewers are used for brewing coffee.  The interface can be opened by pressing **use** on it.

Coffee Brewers need a source of water as fuel.  Currently this is only accepted by the Water Bucket.  Each bucket of water can be used for 6 cups of coffee, with one use being consumed *after* each coffee is brewed.  In order for coffee to start brewing, there needs to be enough fuel, at least 1 coffee ground, and a glass bottle in the output slot.  The coffee will then take 30 seconds to brew.
