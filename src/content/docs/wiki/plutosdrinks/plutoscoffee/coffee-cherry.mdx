The coffee cherry is used to grow a coffea plant

## Obtaining

Found in End City Chests and Plains Village Houses

## Growing

Plant the Coffee Cherry on tilled Farmland, and wait for it to sprout Red Berries on it.  Harvested similar to sweet berries.  Right clicking the fully grown plant will pick 2-3 cherries from the crop and more will grow soon.

## Crafting

![Berry to Bean](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/coffeeberry.png?raw=true)

Put in a crafting table to get a coffee bean
