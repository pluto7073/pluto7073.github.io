Craft a Coffee Brewer to Brew Coffee

![Crafting Recipe](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/coffeebrewer.png?raw=true)

This block is similar to the brewing stand, however instead of blaze powder you use a bucket of water, and it can only hold one bottle at a time.
To use, place a water bucket in the fuel slot, an empty glass bottle in the bottom slot, and coffee grounds of your choice (except espresso grounds) in the top slot.

![Brewing Recipe](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/coffee.png?raw=true)

It will take some time to brew, but if you aren't actively looking at the brewing screen you will be able to tell when it is brewed by looking at the block.
