> *The contents of this page are specific to [Pluto's Coffee Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/plutos-coffee-mod)*

Use the [Coffee Grinder](/wiki/plutosdrinks/plutoscoffee/coffee-grinder) to grind your coffee with the most efficiency.

Place any coffee grounds in the top slot of the **coffee grinder** and after one second it will output 4 of the corresponding grounds.

<img src="https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosCoffeeMod-Fabric/blob/master/images/new/coffee_grinder_ui.png?raw=true" width="500"/>

## The old way

In older versions of the mod *(\<2.2.x)*, you would craft coffee grounds using a stonecutter.  While you can still do this, it will only give you 1 coffee ground for every bean instead of 4, seeing as it is a literal sawblade.
