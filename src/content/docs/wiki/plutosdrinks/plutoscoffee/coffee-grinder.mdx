## Usage
> *Main Article: [Grinding Coffee](/wiki/plutosdrinks/plutoscoffee/grinding-coffee)*

To use the **coffee grinder**, simply place a coffee bean in the top slot.  The **coffee grinder** doesn't require any fuel, and after 1 second, the grinder will output 4 of the corresponding coffee grounds.
