The Drink Workstation is a block used for adding ingredients to various drinks in the series

## Obtaining

## Usage
> *Main Article: [Drink Add-Ins](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosDrinksAPI/wiki/Drink-Add%E2%80%90Ins)*

To use a **Drink Workstation**, simply open the UI using the *use* key.  Then, place the [base item](https://github.com/pluto7073/PlutosDrinksAPI/wiki/Valid-Drink-Bases) in the first slot and the addition item in the second slot, then the result will appear in the 3rd slot.  The item will have a tooltip showing the add-ins and the amount of each.

### The Art of Bartending
In [The Art of Bartending](https://modrinth.com/mod/bartending), you can craft a **Counter Top** using [iron ingots](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ingot).

| Ingredients                                       | Crafting recipe                                                                                                                                                         |
|---------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| [Iron Ingot](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Iron_Ingot) | <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pluto7073/TheArtOfBartending-Fabric/refs/heads/1.20/images/recipes/counter_top.png" width="200" alt="counter top recipe" /> |

The counter top's GUI is identical to that of the Drink Workstation's, and it has the same function.

You can use either the counter top or the drink workstation to add items to bottles of alcohol.  This can be done in order to create other alcoholic drinks.
