MODS

Fly Without Elytra

Fly Without Elytra is a mod that allowes
you to glide like with elytra, but without
elytra. Fireworks have no effect when gliding
though.

PlutosCoffeeMod

PlutosCoffeeMod adds coffee to the game. Grow coffee
berries, peel them to get the beans, roast the beans,
and grind the beans, then craft with water and a glass
bottle and whala! You have coffee!

TeaMod

TeaMod adds tea to the game. Grow Tea. Brew Tea. Drink Tea
Its that simple.
